Data Privacy

Common Security Questions

AMPD collects only the data necessary to operate the platform for your athletic program. This includes basic profile information (name, email, role) and data generated through platform use (check in responses, workout completion, schedule interactions).

We never collect more than what is needed. We never sell student data. We never share data with third party advertisers. Period.

Every user sees only what they are supposed to see. The system enforces strict role based visibility:

  • Athletes see their own data, their own trends, and content from their coaches.
  • Parents see their child's compliance and schedule. They never see raw readiness scores or other athletes' data.
  • Coaches see their team's data only. Head coaches see the full team. Staff members see what the head coach grants access to.
  • Athletic Directors see aggregate program level data across all teams in their organization.

Schools can restrict athlete account creation to approved email domains only. This ensures that only verified students with a valid school email address can join your program on AMPD. This feature is optional and can be configured per organization.

For athletes under 13, AMPD follows the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) guidelines. We support parental consent workflows and minimize data collection for younger users in accordance with federal requirements.

AMPD uses session based authentication with encrypted cookies. Sessions are scoped to the user's organization and role. There are no persistent tokens floating around in local storage or URLs. Sessions expire and can be revoked by administrators.

AMPD does not run ads. We do not use student data for advertising purposes. We do not share data with ad networks or data brokers. Your athletes are not the product.
