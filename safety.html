Common Security Questions

How do you handle student data?

AMPD collects only the data necessary to operate the platform. We never sell student data. We never share data with third-party advertisers. Period.

Role-based access control?

Every user sees only what they are supposed to see. Athletes see their own data. Parents see compliance and schedules. Coaches see their team. ADs see the full program.

Domain email enforcement?

Schools can restrict athlete account creation to approved email domains only. Optional and configurable per organization.

COPPA compliance?

For athletes under 13, AMPD follows COPPA guidelines with parental consent workflows and minimized data collection.

Zero advertising policy?

AMPD does not run ads. We do not use student data for advertising. Your athletes are not the product.
