## Mismatches and Gaps

### 1. Grades product is buried
Grades is a live add-on but only appears as a single bullet in the Terms of Service description of the platform. It is NOT mentioned on:
- Home page
- Platform page
- Any nav or footer
- Any marketing copy

### 2. Coming-soon products completely absent
Counsel, Training Room, and Fuel are not mentioned anywhere on the website. No "coming soon" section, no roadmap, no teaser.

### 3. "Training" ambiguity
The website uses "Training" as a module name (workouts feature). The product catalog has "Training Room" as a separate coming-soon product. These may need differentiation to avoid confusion.

### 4. "5 Platform Modules" stat may need updating
The about.html page states `"5 Platform Modules"`. If Grades is considered a module (it's live), this should be 6. If coming-soon products are eventually added, the number will need further updating.

### 5. NFC wristband check-in
Referenced on index.html (line 198) in the comparison checklist: `"NFC wristband check-in"`. This feature is not elaborated on anywhere else on the site -- no platform page section, no explanation of what it is.

### 6. Terms of Service lists features not on marketing pages
The terms.html "Description of Service" (line 182-191) lists:
- `"Analytics and reporting — aggregated readiness trends and participation data"` -- not highlighted on platform page
- `"Grade eligibility tracking — SIS-integrated academic monitoring"` -- not on platform page
- `"AI-powered workout management"` -- described differently on platform page (just "Training")
