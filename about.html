"Baseball is 90 percent mental. The other half is physical."

So where’s the platform

There are tools for film. Tools for stats. Training programs for every physical skill. But the part everyone agrees matters most? Nothing. Until now.

The Reality

An athlete wakes up and the first thing on their mind is not the game plan. It is the doubt. Am I starting? Did I do enough? What if I make the same mistake again?

They carry that into the car. Into the locker room. Onto the field. By the time they step between the lines, their body is there but their mind checked out an hour ago.

Coaches see it. They can feel when a kid is not right. But they have got a two-hour window and thirty players. There is no system, no tool, no daily touchpoint shaping how these athletes show up mentally.

The problem was never getting them to show up. It is how they show up.

After a tough loss, they go home with nothing but their own thoughts. Before a big game, they are overthinking with no way to reset. Between practices, the mental game has zero support. That is the gap. And it has existed forever.

Our Founder

"I know what it feels like to show up every day and not be right mentally. No athlete should have to figure that out alone."

The Solution

Physical meets digital.
Every single day.

AMPD gives coaches and organizations a way to stay connected to their athletes in the moments that matter most. Not just during practice. Every day.

The Physical

Culture should not live in an app. It should live on them. That is why AMPD lives on a wrist. Every rep, every class, every game, every setback, it is there. A constant physical reminder of who they are, what they represent, and who they are doing it for.

The Digital

One tap delivers mental performance content, daily reinforcement, workouts, and structure. Coaches get the tools to shape not just athletes, but people. No app to download. No friction. Just a tap and a message that meets them where they are.

Our Mission

Bigger than the game.

Coaches are already in a position to shape young lives. But they are limited to the hours they get on the field. AMPD extends their influence into the 22 hours a day they do not have access to.

Your mental is the most important thing you will ever work on in your life. With the right mindset, anything is possible. Without it, talent does not matter.

AMPD gives every organization the platform to build a culture of mental performance. Daily. Consistent. Real. Because the athletes who stay locked in between practices are the ones who show up different when it counts.
