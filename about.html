Built by someone who actually lived it.

AMPD wasn't designed in a boardroom. It was built by a former athlete who lived through the silence, the self-doubt, and the gap between what athletes need and what high school programs actually provide.

Our purpose is simple: give every high school coach the tools to develop their athletes in every aspect of life. Not just physically. Mentally. Emotionally. Every day.

By replacing gut feelings with real data, we help coaches catch what they can't see, follow up on what matters, and build programs that develop athletes — not just win games.

What We Believe

Athletes First

Every feature, every decision starts with the athlete. Not the admin dashboard. Not the report. The kid.

Accessibility

Enterprise tools shouldn't require enterprise budgets. Every high school program deserves the same resources as a D1 school.

Accountability

If an athlete is struggling, someone knows. The system catches what coaches can't see and makes sure every flag gets a follow-up.

The Coach

Coaches do more than lead teams. They're mentors for life. We make that connection easier, more frequent, and make sure no athlete ever feels alone.

Why AMPD

Every day an athlete wakes up, we want to be there getting them AMPD up.

Not just for the game. For life.

That's why we built a mental performance system directly into the platform. So coaches don't just train athletes — they develop them. Every check-in, every mindset upload, every flag that gets followed up on is another moment where an athlete knows someone is in their corner.

AMPD isn't a name. It's what we do.
