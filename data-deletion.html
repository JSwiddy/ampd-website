Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

AMPD lets athletic directors connect their school's Facebook Page and Instagram Business account so the platform can publish approved posts on the school's behalf. This page explains how to disconnect that integration and request deletion of any data AMPD stores about it.

What AMPD Stores When You Connect Meta

When an athletic director connects their school's Meta accounts to AMPD, AMPD stores the following on the school's record:

  • A long-lived Meta access token, encrypted at rest, used only to publish AMPD-approved posts
  • The Facebook Page ID and Instagram Business Account ID
  • The page name and Instagram username, for display in the AMPD dashboard
  • An audit log of every post AMPD published on behalf of the school

AMPD does not store the contents of your personal Facebook profile, your friends list, your private messages, or anything else outside the school's Page / Instagram Business account.

Option 1 — Disconnect from inside AMPD

This is the fastest path. It immediately revokes the stored access token and deletes the connection record from AMPD's database.

  1. Sign in to the AMPD portal at app.liveampd.com
  2. Go to AMPD Social → Connections
  3. Click Disconnect next to the Facebook / Instagram connection
  4. Confirm the disconnect

After this, AMPD can no longer publish to your Meta accounts and the encrypted access token is removed from our database.

Option 2 — Revoke from inside Meta

You can also revoke AMPD's access from your Meta account directly:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Business Integrations
  2. Find AMPD in the list of connected apps
  3. Click Remove

Once revoked from Meta's side, the next time AMPD attempts to use the token, the call will fail and AMPD's system will automatically mark the connection as disconnected.

Option 3 — Request full deletion of all data AMPD holds about you

If you want AMPD to permanently delete all data tied to your account or your school, email privacy@liveampd.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address tied to your AMPD account
  • The name of your school or athletic program
  • What you want deleted (the Meta connection only, your user account, or the entire school's data)

AMPD will confirm receipt within two business days and complete the deletion within 30 days, in accordance with the timelines spelled out in our Privacy Policy.

Questions

For any other questions about how AMPD handles your data, see our Privacy Policy or contact privacy@liveampd.com.
