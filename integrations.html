How It Works

Three steps. No code. No professional services.

The athletic director triggers the setup. The district IT administrator pastes credentials. AMPD does the rest. Most pilots are connected in under thirty minutes.

01

AD invites IT

The athletic director generates a one-time, single-use setup link from inside AMPD and emails it to the district's IT contact.

02

IT pastes credentials

The IT administrator opens the link, picks the district's SIS, pastes the OneRoster API URL and credentials, and tests the connection.

03

AMPD reads the SIS

AMPD pulls the active roster, fetches live class schedules and grades on demand, and computes eligibility. No data is persisted.

No data stored. Grades, GPA, transcripts, and class schedules are live-fetched from the district SIS, rendered in the response, and discarded. AMPD only persists a boolean eligibility flag, not the underlying academic record.
