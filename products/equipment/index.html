Equipment & Inventory

Issued, Returned, and Accounted For

Catalog

Equipment Catalog

Every piece of gear, catalogued

Helmets, jerseys, pads, uniforms, balls, bags. Build a catalog of everything your department owns, organized by sport.

Item-tracked or bulk

High-value gear gets individual asset tags and condition tracking. Consumables are counted in aggregate. Track each the way it makes sense.

Replacement value built in

Set what each item is worth. When gear goes missing or comes back damaged, the dollar figure is already there.

Issue in Seconds

Assign gear to an athlete

Pick the athlete, pick the items, and it's checked out to them. Every issuance is logged against the right name.

Signed acknowledgment

In person or by link

Invoicing

Auto-Invoicing

Unreturned gear bills itself

When equipment comes back damaged or doesn't come back at all, AMPD generates an itemized invoice at replacement value automatically.

Parents pay online

Invoices go to parents and get paid through the same secure Stripe rails as registration. Receipts are automatic.

Reminders run themselves

Due-soon, overdue, and final notices send on their own. You stop being the collections department.

Damaged or lost → Invoiced → Paid online

Clearance & Audits

An athlete with unreturned equipment or an unpaid invoice can be held from clearing for the next season, until it's resolved.

Inventory audits

Run a count and reconcile against what should be on the shelf. Items that can't be found flip to lost automatically.

Know your real inventory

Budget for next year on facts, not guesses. See exactly what you have, what's out, and what's gone.
