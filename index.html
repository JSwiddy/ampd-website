The Daily Check-In

Five metrics. Fifteen seconds. Every day.

Athletes rate their Energy, Stress, Soreness, Focus, and Confidence each morning. Coaches see who is locked in and who needs attention — before practice even starts. Color-coded, streak-tracked, and impossible to miss.

The Mindset Upload

Coach just uploaded your mindset.

A daily 60-second morning ritual delivered straight from the coach. Pattern interrupt, theme word, mindset message, and a mental rep. Building mental strength one day at a time, powered by real team readiness data.

Automatic alerts when an athlete is trending down. A single metric drops two points in a day? Flag. Three consecutive days of decline? Flag. Missed multiple check-ins? Flag. Coaches see it before the athlete even walks through the door.
