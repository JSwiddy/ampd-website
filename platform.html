The Differentiator

The only built-in mental performance system in sports.

Every program has tools for film, stats, and the physical side. AMPD is the first platform that makes the mental game a daily practice.

Five metrics, fifteen seconds, every morning. Coaches see team readiness before anyone walks through the door. Color coded from red to green. Streaks and compliance built in.

The Mindset Upload

A six-layer morning ritual: pattern interrupt, theme word, mindset message, mental rep, check-in bridge, and proof. AI-generated contextual content based on real team readiness data.

Automatic alerts when readiness drops two points in a day, trends decline for three consecutive days, overall readiness falls 20% below average, or an athlete misses three check-ins in a week.
